## ‚ú® Pro Tips

### **For Your Pitch**
1. **Practice** in front of a mirror 10 times
2. **Time** yourself - aim for exactly 60 seconds
3. **Memorize** but don't sound robotic
4. **Show** the live demo (have it ready)
5. **Know** your numbers cold (TAM, CAC, LTV)

### **For Questions**
1. **Listen** fully before answering
2. **Acknowledge** the concern
3. **Answer** with specifics, not generalizations
4. **Tie back** to your competitive advantage
5. **Know** when to say "great question, let me follow up"

### **For Follow-ups**
1. **Within 24 hours** send demo access + financials
2. **References** - have 2-3 ready (customers or advisors)
3. **Roadmap** - show what's next
4. **Team** - be clear about who's doing what
