## üé¨ LIVE DEMO CONFIDENCE TIPS

1. **Practice 5 times** before the call
2. **Slow down** - You'll naturally rush. Speak 30% slower
3. **Pause for reactions** - Give investor 3 seconds to respond
4. **Eyes on investor** - Not the screen
5. **Point, don't read** - Use the demo, don't narrate it
6. **Expect interruptions** - They're good! Answer, then resume
7. **Have a backup plan** - Screenshots if tech fails
8. **End with CTA** - "Can I send you the investor deck?" or "Can we do a pilot?"
